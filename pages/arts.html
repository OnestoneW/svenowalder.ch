You`re the all-singing, all-dancing crap of the world.

The life is a stage and I love and use this stage as often as I can.

I started playing the piano back in 2002, mastered my music skills over the years and added some guitar and percussion instruments to the repetoir.

From the very beginning I had to take everything from this life. My first word was "no", closely chased by "me too", which prepared the path I should go through my life.

After school I started an apprenticeship at Raiffeisenbank Guntalingen-Neunforn in banking and graduated 3 years later in 2012. I wanted a change and started in the same year as a software tester at Raiffeisen Schweiz. Flashed of all the technical stuff, I went deeper and deeper in the ICT environment until I programmed interface tests based on C# where I found my passion for software developement. To prevent boredom, I visited the Stage Art Musical and Theater School (SAMTS) and educated my acting skills off the job.

Regarding to my long civil service, I quitted my job as Testmanager (I was participating in a big frontend project as Qualitymanager) and went into a highschool to fulfill my duty as a teaching assistant.

As all my days on duty were over, I had the wish to learn more about network engineering, so I applied to a job as network supporter at a local internet provider (Init7) where I am working until this day.
